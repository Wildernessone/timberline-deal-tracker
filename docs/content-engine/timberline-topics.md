# Timberline content engine — topic surface

These are **SEEDS, not a finite menu.** Each run: pick a portal (rotate across all 3 so coverage
stays even), then combine a **species/method** with a **topic-type** to form one specific, valuable
article — and INVENT fresh topics in these veins. The surface is huge; never repeat anything already
in the `articles` table (you ran the dedupe). Vary species, method, and topic-type every run so each
portal reads like a deep, real hunting publication, not the same few themes.

## Topic types (apply to any portal/species)
- Buying & fit guides ("how to choose X" — fit/system guides, NOT "best X" listicles)
- Gear-care & maintenance
- Field tactics & skills
- E-scouting + tag/draw strategy
- Fitness & physical prep
- Meat care & field processing
- Seasonal prep (pre-season / in-season / late-season)
- Beginner how-tos
- Deep-dives: optics / boots / packs / layering / sleep systems
- Recurring: "Best <category> deals — <Month Year>" grounded in current live deals

## TIMBERLINE portal (western / backcountry / mountain)
- **Species:** elk, mule deer, bighorn/dall/desert/stone sheep, mountain goat, pronghorn antelope,
  spring & spot-and-stalk black bear, moose; **western turkey here and there** (Merriam's, Rio Grande).
- **Methods:** spot-and-stalk, backpack/pack-in, high-country, DIY public-land, OTC vs draw,
  archery elk, long-range rifle.
- **Angles:** elk calling, glassing systems, e-scouting (OnX/GoHunt), altitude & backcountry fitness,
  packing out meat, water treatment, backcountry sleep systems, binos vs spotting scopes, mountain
  boots, pack frames & load hauling, mountain layering, draw-odds & tag strategy.

## WHITETAIL portal — Treestand Saver (ANYTHING hunted from a treestand, saddle, or elevated setup; eastern/mobile)
- **Species:** whitetail deer; **bear** over bait & from stands/saddles; **hog** over bait/feeders &
  from stands; **turkey here and there** (eastern).
- **Setups & methods:** saddle hunting (saddle, sticks, platform, ropes/lineman's, safety); mobile
  run-and-gun; treestands (hang-on / climber / ladder); **elevated & box/tower blinds**; **feeders &
  bait sites (where legal)**; ground blinds.
- **Angles:** scent control (laundry/storage/field), the rut (pre-rut / peak / post-rut / lockdown),
  stand placement & wind/thermals, public-land hunting, trail-camera strategy, rattling & calling,
  mock scrapes, late-season cold, climbing & harness safety, food plots.

## WATERFOWL portal — Duck Blind Deals
- Ducks vs geese; decoy spreads (puddle ducks, divers, field geese, early vs late season); blinds
  (layout, A-frame, boat, pit, natural); duck & goose calling; waders & wader care; retriever/dog work;
  scouting & running traffic; public-water tactics; motion / jerk rigs / spinners; shotgun chokes &
  nontox loads for waterfowl; cold & wet-weather gear; boat & blind setup.
